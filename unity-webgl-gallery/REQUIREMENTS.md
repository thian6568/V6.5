# Requirements (unity-webgl-gallery)

1. Load environment metadata for at least 50 curated environments.
2. Fetch approved artwork records from backend-core.
3. Assign artworks to wall anchors based on environment assignments.
4. Respect visibility and approval states from backend.
5. Support smooth navigation controls and interaction prompts.
6. Avoid duplicate upload/storage workflows in Unity.
7. Expose NFC/QR-ready metadata hooks for artwork interactions.
